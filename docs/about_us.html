About us

The company Foster Facility s.r.o. was founded in 2018 as a service unit focused on cleaning activities and small renovations. In 2020, Foster Facility s.r.o. retail services and employment agency services from Foster Bau a.s. Thus, Foster Facility continues to successfully provide quality services in the retail segment and employment agency.

We are ready to listen to your needs and design a tailor-made solution for you. We are building a transparent company where you will always know where you are investing your money.

Our vision is to build a company that will be a reliable and respected partner on the European market.
