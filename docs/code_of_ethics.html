Code of ethics

Code of Ethics of Foster Facility s.r.o. it contains important rules of conduct to help us answer correctly and appropriately the ethical and legal questions we encounter in strategic considerations and decision-making processes.

  • 1.COMPLIANCE

    • Adherence to applicable legal and other regulations (compliance) is the basis of our decision-making and actions in doing business and is firmly embedded in our corporate culture.

  • 2.PROHIBITION OF CORRUPTION

    • FOSTER FACILITY is against corruption and bribery. Business practices that use unfair means are not tolerated. Employees of the FOSTER FACILITY may not offer their business partners any benefits that lead to a violation of objective and fair business decision-making or could even create such an impression, nor may they receive or accept such benefits from them.

  • 3.PREVENTION OF CONFLICT OF INTEREST

    • At FOSTER FACILITY, business decisions are made exclusively in the best interest of the company. Potential conflicts of interest with private affairs or other economic or any other activities, including those involving close persons or organizations, must be nipped in the bud. If it nevertheless occurs, such a case must be dealt with in accordance with legal regulations and applicable internal guidelines. A condition for such a procedure is transparent informing of the superior about the conflict of interests.

  • 4.MONEY LAUNDERING

    • No employee may, either alone or jointly with others, engage in conduct that violates domestic or foreign anti-money laundering regulations. In case of doubt regarding the admissibility of transactions involving the transfer of cash, the relevant finance department should be contacted as soon as possible.

  • 5.DONATIONS AND OTHER PROPERTY BENEFITS

    • Employees of the FOSTER FACILITY may not request, accept, offer or provide - whether directly or indirectly - unauthorized property benefits in the form of monetary payments, gifts or any other contributions or performance during their work activities. This applies in relation to individuals, businesses as well as official bodies or other institutions. In particular, no benefits of any kind may be offered or provided to official representatives of domestic or foreign institutions. The only exceptions are ordinary occasional or promotional gifts, entertainment or other occasional pleasures that are demonstrably of little value.

  • 6.INSIDER INFORMATION

    • Employees may not use the information obtained in the course of their work for their own purposes or in the interest of third parties. If employees receive information about the internal affairs of customers or the FOSTER FACILITY company that may affect the stock exchange listing, they must treat this information as confidential and must not pass it on to third parties. The use of this information for your own or third parties' purposes is strictly prohibited.

  • 7.CONFIDENTIALITY AND DATA PROTECTION

    • Much of FOSTER FACILITY's business information is confidential or proprietary and therefore subject to confidentiality. This does not apply if disclosure of such information has been approved by FOSTER FACILITY or disclosure is required by law or regulation. The obligation of confidentiality applies in particular to intellectual property. This includes trade secrets, patents, trademarks and copyrights, but also business and marketing plans, proposals, business documents, salary details and any other undisclosed financial data and reports. All personal information about employees, customers, business partners and suppliers, as well as about other third parties, is used by FOSTER FACILITY with due care, maintaining the principle of confidentiality and strict compliance with data protection legislation. Maximum care must be taken to protect this information.

  • 8.PERSONAL CONDUCT

    • FOSTER FACILITY and its employees act correctly and with respect in dealings with customers, suppliers, co-workers and other persons. There is no violation of human dignity and privacy. All employees are obliged to act openly, fairly and with respect in their interactions with each other and strive to find a common solution to the problems that have arisen.

  • 9.PROHIBITION OF DISCRIMINATION

    • Diskriminace na základě pohlaví nebo sexuální orientace, věku, vyznání nebo smýšlení, rasové nebo etnické příslušnosti, národnostního nebo sociálního původu nebo postižení pracovníků je zakázána. V mezinárodním styku je nutno respektovat zvyklosti jiných národů. Zaměstnanci i vedoucí pracovníci jsou povinni aktivně vystupovat proti diskriminaci.

  • 10.IMPLEMENTATION AND CONTROL OF COMPLIANCE

    • The rules contained in this code of ethics are among the pillars of FOSTER FACILITY's corporate culture. Uniform adherence to these principles is imperative. Every employee is responsible for them. If any employee has questions or comments about the points mentioned in this code of ethics, or is aware of a possible violation of the rules of conduct contained in it, he should immediately contact his supervisor with such a matter. It may also do so anonymously or on the basis of confidentiality.

  • 11.LIABILITY

    • All employees and management members of FOSTER FACILITY are bound by the rules of this code of ethics. The Code of Ethics contains the values, principles and rules of conduct on which FOSTER FACILITY is based in its business activities. The goal of the company's management is to comply with ethical standards and create a work environment that supports integrity, respect and fair behavior.
